Thomchelik T. Tunca


Hello! My name is Thomchelik Tunca and I am a Turkish American student studying at the George
Washington University. My family and I currently live in Chicago, IL. I am currently a Bachelor of Science
student in Computer Science looking to graduate Spring 2016, and have accepted emplyment at Infosys.
My duel citizenship allows me to see a whole different part of the planet that most Americans do not have the chance to see. That being said, I see a problem in both parts of the planet, and that is air pollution, namely CO2 pollution
from burning fossil fuels.  After seeing how bad Istanbul is since I was a little boy I always wanted
to do my part and help clean the world up. I always tried to pick up garbage on the street, or joined environmental
clubs since I can remember. Then senior design came along and gave me a chance to help the environment in a whole
new way; technologically. Other interests I have include friends, family, skiing, and sports.
